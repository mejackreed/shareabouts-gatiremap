About

To use, click the button "Add a Place" and then fill out the brief form. Your tire report will be added to the map.

This is used for local Georgia communities to collect data on tires illegally dumped in their neighborhoods. Illegally dumped tires pose a public health threat and can become breeding grounds for disease-carrying mosquitoes and rodents.
